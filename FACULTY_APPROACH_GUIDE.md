### Q1: "How much time will this take to develop?"
**A:** "The basic version is already 80% complete. I can have a working demo ready within 2 weeks for your review."

### Q2: "Will it be difficult for faculty to use?"
**A:** "Not at all. It's designed to be simpler than the current paper method. Just search student by roll number and click 'Mark Late' - done in 5 seconds."

### Q3: "What about data security?"
**A:** "The system uses industry-standard security with password protection, encrypted data, and role-based access. Only authorized faculty can access student records."

### Q4: "Can we get reports?"
**A:** "Yes! You can generate reports by date, department, year, or student. Export to Excel for administrative purposes."

### Q5: "What if students come late together? Will it take long?"
**A:** "There's a quick-add feature. You can mark multiple students in less than a minute."

**A:** "I'll provide complete documentation, training, and source code. The system is hosted on cloud platforms with 99.9% uptime, requiring minimal maintenance."
